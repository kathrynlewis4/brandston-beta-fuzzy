PROFILE

FIRM PROFILE


Brandston Partnership Inc., an award winning architectural lighting design firm, has completedover 4,000 project in North America, South America, Europe, Asia and Australia since its founding in 1966. Our professional staffs of twelve in New York and about sixty in Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen, Chengdu and Singapore is trained in architecture, interior design, engineering or theater, with primary interest in architectural lighting design. BPI has been selected as the lighting designer for some of most prestigious projects built in the last half century. The client list and number of honors BPI has received give testimony to the quality of the design work that we have contributed to this projects.

We have an extraordinary diversity of experience at every scale and virtually every project type. Our work ranges from jewel-box boutiques, intimate cafes and small apartments, to insightful historic restorations, technically demanding aquaria and major city planning projects.

At BPI, we use words, pictures, diagrams, sketches, drawings, models and mock-ups to develop and convey our ideas and designs. We explore our clientsâ€™ design challenges thoroughly, with sensitivity and ingenuity, resulting in integrated, creative and appropriate solutions that meet their specific lighting needs.

We offer a full range of lighting design services in accordance with established standards and procedures, including schematic designs, lighting calculations, renderings, custom fixtures, daylighting design, detailed design, bid or tender documents, contract administration and site supervision.

Our realized projects demonstrate both an awareness of human issues and of design sensitivity; our working philosophy emphasizes collaboration and client input combined with dedicated personal service.

We maintain electronic networks that allow us to work in AutoCAD, the calculation program AGI, and the usual array of utilitarian office programs, including extensive PowerPoint presentations that include both hand sketches and computer-based images of lighting effects made over architectural renderings. We can turn daytime exterior renderings into night-time views, and provide interior views showing realistic expectations of what spaces will look like when built. All this also allows us to work collaboratively between our offices and with clients that are thousands of miles, and many time-zones apart.

Notable projects include:

  • Statue of Liberty
  • Kuala Lumpur City Center
  • American Museum of Natural History
  • International Finance Centre II
  • MGM City Center Circulation
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport
  • China World Trade Center 3A
